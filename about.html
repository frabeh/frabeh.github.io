I'm a UX designer passionate about creating meaningful, intuitive experiences.
My name is Francesca Behling.

I'm a risk-taker, problem-solver, and former project manager passionate about making experiences easier for people.

I like to tackle challenges and solve the seemingly unsolvable, and I like most to do this for other people. In my work life, this means that the products I've designed are intuitive and easy to figure out without an instruction manual.

It's important to me to not only create a functional product, but also one that elicits a particular feeling from the user. Depending on the product, I want my users to feel more in control of their work or a part of an embraceable community or like they could take on the world.

I believe that there is no limit to the experiences we can create, and want to be on the front lines of the new frontiers being explored.

In my personal life, my ability to solve problems makes me a great Rummikub player. It also makes me a great recipe resource for my friends, since in my instructions I tend to answer questions they didn't even know they would have.

Take a look at my resume or contact me. I'd love to help you bring your products to life.
