I'm a risk-taker, problem-solver, and former project manager making experiences easier for people.
My name is Francesca Behling.

I like to tackle challenges and solve the seemingly unsolvable. In my work life, this means that the products I've designed are intuitive and easy to use without an instruction manual.

It's important to me to create products that are functional and elicit a particular feeling from the user. Depending on the product, I want my users to feel more in control of their work, like they are a part of an embraceable community, or like they could take on the world.

I believe that there is no limit to the experiences we can create, and want to be on the front lines of the new frontiers being explored.

In my personal life, my ability to solve problems makes me a great Rummikub player. It also makes me a great recipe resource, since in my instructions I answer questions people didn't even know they would have.

Take a look at my resume or contact me. I'd love to help bring your products to life.
