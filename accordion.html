Accordion

Project Manager & Designer

I was brought onto this project as a consultant for Gist Digital, the incubator working with Accordion. At its early stages, Accordion was an app designed to facilitate communication within and between construction teams. Gist Digital had produced a very early MVP of the app and had just started a closed beta test when my team started on the project.

With user testing and industry research, my team was able to hone in on the most appropriate target audience, iterate on the app, and design a corresponding web application to make communication and document sharing easier. As project manager, I was responsible for leading a team of four-- one marketer and three designers, including myself-- thorugh this process.

User Testing

The target audience for the MVP in beta was the full contruction industry, including everyone from small individual contractors working on residential projects to large industrial construction firms. Very limited user testing had been done in the development of the MVP, so my team's first project was to test how the target audience responded to the app.

Due to the closed nature of the beta test and the difficulty involved with creating new user profiles, the team elected to conduct in-person interviews with the MVP and online interviews using the Marvel prototype provided to us. The group as a whole identified the most important information we would need out of each interview. At this stage, I was responsible for contacting and following up with industry members such as contractors, painters, landscapers, and regulatory consultants from variously-sized companies and conducted some of the interviews.

<

We were able to determine that the app needed additional features to be useful. These needs, however, varied by user. In the end we realized that the two main groups who would most benefit from the app were a) small construction-related companies (contractors, painters, etc in companies of around 50 or fewer people) and b) homeowners wanting to head a DIY project or renovation.
